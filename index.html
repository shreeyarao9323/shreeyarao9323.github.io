About me

Welcome to Focus â€“ Every Picture is a Story, I am Shreeya Rao, a young photographer based out in Chicago, U.S.A. I was born and raised in Hyderabad, India, Over the years and through my experiences, I have learnt not just to see with my eyes, but to feel them and develop it with own style. My work focuses on capturing the 'art of seeing' in a way that chronicles concepts such as color, contrast, light, and reality.

Holding a computer science degree never did it occur to me that one day, my heart would stir with such a passion that it would send me on another path entirely. But looking through the lens of my first camera, it became clear to me that it was my calling to document life, the memories we make, and the moments we share. The world we live in continually inspires me, whether it is new landscapes or charming cities and towns across the globe. My time is now mostly spent chasing images all over the world to capture, document, and get the most from this amazing life one frame at a time.

If you ask me, each image tells, in its own unique way, a captivating story. Taking pictures lends me an opportunity to capture a specific moment in time that, with the right photographer, can be conveyed infinitely. My secret is being able to see the whole picture in natural light in a way others may not. As Elliott Erwitt said eloquently, "The whole point of taking a picture is so that you don't have to explain things with words!"
